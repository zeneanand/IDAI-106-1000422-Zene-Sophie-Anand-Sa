# Project Title: Mindful Work App Prototype
**Scenario:** Build Mindful Work App Prototype for Stress Free Offices [cite: 16]

## Stage 1: Empathize & Define
### Objective
To understand the stress experiences of employees and identify opportunities for improvement[cite: 29].

### Problem Statement
"How might we design a system that offers instant, personalized relaxation for employees, reducing stress without disrupting workflow?" [cite: 50]

### User Personas
I developed 10 detailed personas representing a range of employees across age, job roles, and stress levels.
*Full details can be found in the /Docs folder.*

1. **Priya (Intern, 24):** Stress from adapting to a new environment; needs music-based relaxation[cite: 45].
2. **Aarav (Marketing Manager, 35):** High anxiety before presentations; prefers short meditation breaks[cite: 44].
3. **Riya (IT Professional, 28):** Experiences daily headaches and stress from back-to-back meeting schedules[cite: 43].
4. **Sameer (HR Executive, 40):** Struggles with a heavy workload; prefers private, quiet spaces for decompression[cite: 46].
5. **Mr. Mehta (Senior Director, 55):** Physical strain from long hours; requires ergonomic and light-based therapy.
6. **Sanya (Remote-Hybrid Worker, 30):** Feels isolated and lacks routine; needs guided focus and connection sessions.
7. **Leo (Creative Lead, 33):** Suffers from "creative block" under pressure; seeks sensory inspiration via aroma and light.
8. **David (Late-Shift Security Head, 48):** Disrupted sleep cycles; needs mood-sync lighting to regulate energy levels.
9. **Anika (New Parent/Analyst, 31):** Sleep-deprived and multi-tasking; needs 2-minute "micro-calm" sessions.
10. **Dr. Lin (Introverted Researcher, 50):** Overstimulated by open-office noise; seeks total sensory deprivation in a pod.
