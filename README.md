# Stage 2: Ideation Report

## Core Problem Statement
"How might we design a system that offers instant, personalized relaxation for employees, reducing stress without disrupting workflow?"

## 1. Design Strategy & Brainstorming
The ideation process focused on bridging the gap between high-pressure office tasks and mental wellbeing. Using **Affinity Mapping** and **HMW (How Might We)** prompts, I identified five compulsory features that leverage Artificial Intelligence to provide scalable, inclusive, and effective stress relief.

---

## 2. The 5 Compulsory Features & Justification

### 1. MoodSync AI (AI-Curated Pod Settings)
* **How it works:** This feature uses AI to analyze real-time biometric data (heart rate, voice tone) or manual user input to adjust the pod’s lighting, aroma diffusers, and spatial audio.
* **Justification:** This directly solves the **sensory overload** experienced by **Dr. Lin** (Researcher) and helps regulate the **circadian rhythm disruption** for **David** (Security Head).

### 2. Breathe with Me (Guided Meditation)
* **How it works:** A responsive UI that offers 2–5 minute audio-visual breathing exercises synced with on-screen haptic prompts.
* **Justification:** Addresses the **pre-meeting anxiety** of **Aarav** (Manager) and fulfills **Anika’s** (New Parent/Analyst) critical need for "micro-calm" sessions that don't consume much time.

### 3. Pod Scheduler (Booking System)
* **How it works:** A friction-free calendar interface that allows users to reserve a Mindful Pod. It includes a "Silent Entry" QR code feature.
* **Justification:** Eliminates the **social anxiety** and "queue stress" identified for **Priya** (Intern) and **Sameer** (HR Executive), ensuring privacy is guaranteed.

### 4. Post-Session Journal (Reflection Space)
* **How it works:** A secure, digital space where users can log their mental state or "vent" via voice-to-text AI which then identifies positive emotional trends.
* **Justification:** Helps **Leo** (Creative Lead) track and overcome **creative blocks** by identifying the specific sensory environments that trigger his best ideas.

### 5. Stress Tracking Dashboard (Visual Progress)
* **How it works:** A data-rich dashboard that visualizes stress trends, recovery scores, and session history over days, weeks, or months.
* **Justification:** Provides the **data-backed proof** required by **Mr. Mehta** (Senior Director) and **Riya** (IT Pro) to validate that their "rest time" is actually improving their professional performance.
